About us

All Hands Active helps you take the next step to completing a project. We provide tools and supplies, teach classes, foster community, and work to enable the public to do cool things. Working on a project? Stop down and use our tools. Want to learn how to build furniture? Take a class. Have an idea and not sure where to start? Get connected with our makers!

Visit and meet other makers.

Sign up for 24/7 membership or use our space and many tools for FREE during public hours.

Volunteer with us! Contact operations@allhandsactive.org for opportunities
